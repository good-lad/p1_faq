PARCEL.ONE – Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is PARCEL.ONE?

PARCEL.ONE is a cross-border logistics platform based in Germany, created to simplify international shipping for online retailers. It consolidates packages and intelligently routes them through a network of over 100 global delivery partners. By doing so, it reduces shipping costs, increases delivery reliability, and improves customer satisfaction across more than 200 destinations worldwide.

2. How does PARCEL.ONE work?

PARCEL.ONE operates as an intelligent shipping hub. Merchants send their parcels to a centralized location in Germany. There, each parcel is scanned, sorted, and matched with the best-performing local carrier in the recipient country based on cost, speed, and success rate. Smart data-driven routing ensures parcels are delivered faster, at lower costs, with end-to-end tracking and customs compliance built in.

3. Which countries does PARCEL.ONE ship to?

We currently support shipping to over 200 countries and territories, including all EU nations, the UK, USA, Canada, China, Japan, South Korea, Australia, UAE, and many more. Our platform dynamically matches each parcel with optimal last-mile carriers in these regions, such as USPS, Royal Mail, La Poste, or Correos. For a full destination list and carrier matrix, please contact our support team.

4. What shipping services does PARCEL.ONE offer?

PARCEL.ONE offers a range of international eCommerce shipping solutions:

  • Tracked and untracked parcel delivery
  • Delivery to door, locker, or pickup point (depending on destination)
  • DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) and DDU (Delivered Duty Unpaid) services
  • Returns management, including prepaid return labels
  • Smart routing, bulk upload, and shipment data enrichment via API

5. Can I use PARCEL.ONE if I have a small shop?

Yes. PARCEL.ONE works with businesses of all sizes—from small independent webshops to large marketplaces. There's no minimum volume required to get started. Our onboarding is simple, and pricing scales with your volume so even small shops can benefit from optimized international delivery at competitive rates.

6. How can I integrate PARCEL.ONE with my shop?

You can connect to PARCEL.ONE using multiple methods:

  • Prebuilt plugins for Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento
  • A robust REST API for custom integrations
  • CSV upload via a secure web dashboard
Our integration process includes technical support and API documentation to ensure smooth setup and operation.

7. Does PARCEL.ONE offer shipment tracking?

Yes. Every tracked shipment gets a unique tracking link that consolidates status updates from the first mile to last-mile delivery. This unified tracking system supports multiple languages, is mobile-friendly, and can be embedded into your eCommerce platform or shipping notifications to customers.

8. How fast is international delivery?

Delivery times depend on destination and service level:

  • EU deliveries: 2–5 business days
  • North America: 5–8 business days
  • Asia, Australia, Rest of World: 7–12 business days
Our dynamic routing system ensures each shipment takes the fastest and most cost-effective path available, minimizing delays due to customs or local disruptions.

9. What if a parcel is lost or damaged?

In the rare case of a lost or damaged shipment, you can file a claim through our client dashboard within 7 business days. Required documents include proof of value (invoice), photos of damage (if applicable), and tracking info. Our support team processes claims promptly, and compensation follows the agreed service level terms.

10. Does PARCEL.ONE handle returns?

Yes. We provide flexible international returns solutions:

  • Prepaid return labels for customers
  • Local drop-off points in major markets
  • Return consolidation hubs for cost-effective processing
  • API support for automating returns
These services can be tailored per destination and customer flow.

11. What does shipping cost?

Pricing is customized based on destination, weight/size, delivery speed, and monthly shipping volume. We offer flexible rate tiers and a personalized onboarding consultation to match your business needs. Contact our sales team or use our online rate request tool to get a quote.

12. How is customs handled?

PARCEL.ONE supports both DDP and DDU models depending on your product and destination. We assist with:

  • Automated HS code assignment
  • Commercial invoice generation
  • CN22/CN23 forms and digital customs pre-clearance
  • Compliance with ICS2, EU VAT, and destination-specific import rules
This helps reduce delays, avoid rejections, and improve delivery reliability.

13. What makes PARCEL.ONE different from traditional carriers?

PARCEL.ONE is not a carrier—it’s a data-driven logistics orchestrator. Instead of locking you into a single network, we evaluate each shipment and dynamically assign it to the best-performing local delivery partner. This results in faster deliveries, fewer returns, higher customer satisfaction, and better tracking. Traditional carriers offer fixed routes; PARCEL.ONE offers smart routing for cross-border scale.
