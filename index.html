PARCEL.ONE – Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is PARCEL.ONE?

PARCEL.ONE is a logistics and technology company based in Germany, specializing in cross-border shipping solutions for e-commerce businesses.

2. How does PARCEL.ONE work?

PARCEL.ONE collects parcels in bulk from merchants and optimally routes them through a multi-carrier system using smart data analysis, resulting in faster, cost-effective, and trackable international deliveries.

3. Which countries does PARCEL.ONE ship to?

PARCEL.ONE ships to over 200 destinations including all EU countries, North America, Asia, Australia, and more. Check our full destination matrix for specifics.

4. What shipping services does PARCEL.ONE offer?

Tracked/untracked shipping, pickup point delivery, customs-prepared delivery (DDP/DDU), returns logistics, and label generation via API.

5. Can I use PARCEL.ONE if I have a small shop?

Yes, we support businesses of all sizes with no minimum parcel volume required. Pricing is volume-based.

6. How can I integrate PARCEL.ONE with my shop?

Via plugins (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento), REST API, or CSV upload portal.

7. Does PARCEL.ONE offer shipment tracking?

Yes, end-to-end tracking is available via a unified tracking page that consolidates carrier data.

8. How fast is international delivery?

Europe: 2–5 days, North America: 5–8 days, Asia/ROW: 7–12 days. Customs and holidays may affect delivery.

9. What if a parcel is lost or damaged?

Submit a claim within 7 business days via our support portal. Provide proof of value and photos where applicable.

10. Does PARCEL.ONE handle returns?

Yes. We provide return labels, drop-off options, and consolidation services tailored per destination.

11. What does shipping cost?

Costs depend on destination, size, service level, and monthly volume. Contact us or use the price calculator for a quote.

12. How is customs handled?

We support DDP and DDU, and assist with HS codes, invoices, and pre-clearance data submission.

13. What makes PARCEL.ONE different from traditional carriers?

We optimize delivery per destination, offer multi-carrier tracking, and focus solely on cross-border logistics.
