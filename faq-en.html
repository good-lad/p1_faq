PARCEL.ONE – Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is PARCEL.ONE?

PARCEL.ONE is a German logistics company specializing in international shipping for online retailers. By connecting to over 100 delivery partners worldwide, PARCEL.ONE ensures your packages are delivered reliably, quickly, and cost-effectively to over 200 countries.

2. How does PARCEL.ONE work?

Online retailers send their packages to a central warehouse in Germany. There, they are scanned, consolidated, and routed via algorithms to the best shipping partner in the destination country. The goal is to shorten delivery times and reduce returns through intelligent routing.

3. Which countries does PARCEL.ONE ship to?

Shipping is available to over 200 countries, including all EU states, the UK, the USA, Canada, China, Australia, and many more. Delivery is carried out by established local partners such as La Poste, Royal Mail, USPS, or Correos.

4. What shipping services does PARCEL.ONE offer?

Our services include:

  • Tracked and untracked shipments
  • Delivery to home, parcel locker, or pickup location
  • DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) and DDU (Delivered Duty Unpaid)
  • Return options with labels
  • API-supported data connection for tracking & customs

5. Can small online shops use PARCEL.ONE?

Yes. There are no minimum volumes. Our scalable solutions are ideal for startups as well as established shops. You benefit from transparent pricing and easy onboarding.

6. How can I integrate PARCEL.ONE with my shop system?

You have several options:

  • Ready-made plugins for Shopify, WooCommerce, or Magento
  • REST API for custom integrations
  • Manual CSV upload in the dashboard

7. Is shipment tracking available?

Yes. All tracked shipments receive a tracking number with real-time status on a unified tracking page – optimized for mobile and multilingual display.

8. How fast is international shipping?

  • Within Europe: 2–5 business days
  • North America: 5–8 business days
  • Worldwide: 7–12 business days
Delivery times depend on destination country, local holidays, and customs processing.

9. What happens if a package is lost or damaged?

You can file a claim within 7 business days of delivery issues. Photos (if damaged), an invoice, and the tracking number are required. Claims are processed promptly.

10. Are returns supported?

Yes. You can generate return labels. Customers drop off returns locally. Packages are consolidated and regularly returned to you. Automated return processes via API are also possible.

11. What does shipping with PARCEL.ONE cost?

Prices vary by destination, weight, service level, and shipping volume. We provide personalized quotes. Contact our sales team or use our price calculator.

12. How is customs clearance handled?

We support DDP & DDU including:

  • Automatic HS code assignment
  • Creation of customs forms (CN22, CN23)
  • Pre-submission of data according to ICS2 / EU regulations
Our processes ensure fast and compliant import clearance.

13. What makes PARCEL.ONE different from traditional carriers?

PARCEL.ONE is not a shipping carrier but a logistics network with intelligent routing. Instead of a fixed route, we use the best local partner per shipment. This means fewer returns, faster delivery, and better tracking – all without switching systems.
